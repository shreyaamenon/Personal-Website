HelmNet Safety Image Classifier

Developed an image classification model to categorize images into two distinct classes: workers wearing safety helmets (With Helmet) and workers not wearing safety helmets (Without Helmet). This project focused on improving workplace safety monitoring by leveraging deep learning to automatically detect helmet usage in images. Using a convolutional neural network (CNN), the model was trained on a curated dataset of labeled images, incorporating techniques like data augmentation and transfer learning to improve performance and handle varied lighting, angles, and environments. The system aims to assist organizations in enforcing safety compliance, reducing workplace hazards, and ensuring that safety protocols are consistently followed in real time.
